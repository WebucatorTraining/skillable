Exercise 1 – Publish your project to Power BI

To move our attention over to Power BI (Online) where our report is to be hosted and shared, we will first need to publish our report. This is a quick process; however, we will need to make sure Power BI Desktop is signed in as the correct account before we can publish to a workspace.

  1. Open the Power BI project House Rules Sales Analysis
     
  2. At the top right of Power BI Desktop is the Sign in section, please do one of the following:
     
  1. If your name appears here, click on your name to confirm this is the account that you want to publish your work to (if not sign our and sign in with the account used for this course)
     
  2. If you see Sign in click on Sign in then enter your email associated with this training course and click Sign in once more, then enter your password, and click Sign in one last time


     
  1. Once signed in, within the Home tab of the ribbon, click on Publish in the Share category


     
  2. Ensure My Workspace is selected and choose Select, the report will start to publish


     
  3. Once published you will get a confirmation, click Got it


     
  4. Close Power BI Desktop, click Save when prompted
     
  5. Lastly, if you do not have a private web browser window open with access to your Microsoft 365 tenant, please follow these steps on page 8 of this module to sign back in, once ready, continue with Exercise 2 on page 94.
