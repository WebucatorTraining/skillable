Lab 3 – Starting a project in Power BI Desktop

A new requirement has come your way at House Rules Board Games. A group of stake holders would like to gather more insights into their transactional database using intelligent reporting. They have provided a series of grain statements for the report to include, and you have spoken to your IT team about accessing the data.

Because you have discussed the requirements with all the relevant parties, we will now create a new project file using Power BI Desktop. We can then connect to the relevant data sources required for our data model and design a series of queries to refine the data. Once ready we will review the relationships that we have created in our model.
